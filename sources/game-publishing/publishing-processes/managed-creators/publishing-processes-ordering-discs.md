# Order discs for manufacture

[!INCLUDE [reminder](../../includes/managed-creators-only-feature.md)]

When you're ready to order discs for manufacture, check that you have completed the following steps.
 
 1. Submitted [royalty tiers](../../how-to/how-to-submit-royalty-tiers.md) and received approval for them.
 2. Submitted all required [packaging artwork](publishing-processes-disc-packaging.md) and received approval for it.
 3. Submitted a [test disc order](publishing-processes-disc-and-digital-certification.md#disc-based-games-only-making-a-disc-request) using an approved game package, and received Xbox Certification approval for the test discs.
 4. Submitted approval for the disc binary to be [released to manufacture (RTM)](publishing-processes-rtm.md) and received confirmation of RTM.
 5. Reviewed and followed all policies that are documented in the [Xbox Publisher Guide](https://aka.ms/xboxpublisherguide).

## Disc ordering process
For detailed guidance about the process and requirements to place a disc order with an Authorized Replicator, read the **Xbox Publisher Manufacturing Guide**, available in the [Xbox Publisher Guide](https://aka.ms/xboxpublisherguide).

## Authorized Replicators
Only the Authorized Replicators (ARs) you select when creating a disc order in Partner Center are permitted to replicate your discs. You must review, evaluate, and select an appropriate AR that meets your needs. Your Microsoft contacts can’t communicate with the AR on your behalf. For more information about ARs, read the **Xbox Publisher Manufacturing Guide**, available in the [Xbox Publisher Guide](https://aka.ms/xboxpublisherguide).

If you need to transfer your binary ID to additional ARs, follow the reordering guidance on [How to create and manage discs](../../how-to/how-to-request-and-manage-discs.md).

## Microsoft samples requirements
As part of your publishing agreement with Microsoft, you might have to provide samples for each Finished Product Unit (FPU) you produce. For information about the required number and types of FPU Samples by region, refer to your publishing agreement with Microsoft and the **Xbox Console Samples** document available in the [Xbox Publisher Guide](https://aka.ms/xboxpublisherguide).
