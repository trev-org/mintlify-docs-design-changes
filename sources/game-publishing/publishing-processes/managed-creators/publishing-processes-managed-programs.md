# Curated programs available for Managed Creators

[!INCLUDE [reminder](../../includes/managed-creators-only-feature.md)]

Microsoft operates a selection of curated programs that you might be able to participate in with your products. For more information, review the guidance in this topic and speak to your Microsoft contacts.

## Discounting and sales

Microsoft manages different discount programs and sales that you might be able to participate in. These programs include Tentpole Sales, Thematic Collection Sales, Weekly Sales, Publisher Sales, and Regional Sales. You might also be able to participate in targeted offers (for example, discounting one product based on ownership of another) or flexible offers (for example, discounting a bundle of products when purchased together). 

Discounting can only be configured by Microsoft. Speak to your Microsoft contacts if you’re interested in participating in any of these scenarios. 

## Free Play Days 

Free Play Days allow potential players to try out your game for free and see if they like it. They’re a great opportunity for you to showcase your game, attract new players and grow your community. If you’re interested in participating in Free Play Days, speak to your Microsoft contacts. 

## Game Pass

Game Pass provides players with a curated catalog of games to play for a monthly subscription fee. For more information, see [Xbox Game Pass | Xbox](https://www.xbox.com/xbox-game-pass).

If you're registered with the ID@Xbox program and want to submit your game for Game Pass consideration, go to [Xbox.com/Publish](https://www.xbox.com/publish) and navigate to **Pitch your game for deal consideration.** If you're not registered with ID@Xbox, speak to your Microsoft contacts to have your game considered for Game Pass.

## Gaming Sustainability

At Xbox, our commitment to our players and the industry is to reduce the impact that gaming has on the environment. With a gaming community of 3.1 billion players worldwide, Microsoft, alongside game creators, can drive positive change. For information about Gaming Sustainability, see [Gaming Sustainability](/gaming/sustainability/sustainability-overview). On this page, you'll find information about:

 1. Power Monitor for PIX to show real time power consumption on devkits
 2. Case studies from titles that have already released energy saving features (lots more to come)
 3. The Energy Efficiency Essentials which is a first release of suggestions written by studios, for studios. Current collaborators have commented they were able to deliver features in only a matter of days with only 1-2 engineers.
 4. Free Certification sustainability reports showing individual in-game power consumption, and compared to industry averages for each in-game area.
 5. Dashboards showing us the global average power consumption of any title on the platform (providing it’s received sufficient gaming activity). 

## Xbox Insider Program

The Xbox Insider Program is a powerful service that allows you to flight your game or app in the RETAIL sandbox, and can be utilized for development, testing, or marketing purposes.

With the Xbox Insider Program you can:

- Quickly and securely deploy your game/app or content update on Xbox consoles or Xbox on PC at any phase of development (including post release)
- Target the audience you want, from a few studio personnel to millions of external users, and easily expand over time
- Allow participants to quickly join, download, and play from anywhere in the world
- Flight to 50,000 or fewer external users with just a minimal Certification review
- Get actionable feedback with built-in tools

For more information, see [Xbox Insider Program flighting](publishing-processes-xbox-insider-flighting.md).
