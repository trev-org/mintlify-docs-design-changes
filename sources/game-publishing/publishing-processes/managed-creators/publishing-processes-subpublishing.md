# Sub-publishing

[!INCLUDE [reminder](../../includes/managed-creators-only-feature.md)]

Sub-publishing permits another Creator that holds a valid publishing agreement with Microsoft to publish your digital product in specific markets (digital sub-publishing) or as a physical release (physical sub-publishing).

## Forms for sub-publishing agreements
When you're working with another Creator to sub-publish a game digitally or physically, you must complete and submit an Xbox Sub-Publishing Form. The primary publisher that submitted the [Game Concept](publishing-processes-game-concepts.md) is responsible for initiating this process. Forms for both digital and physical, or physical-only sub-publishing are available in the [Xbox Publisher Guide](https://aka.ms/xboxpublisherguide) in the **Forms** section. If you don't have access, speak to your Microsoft contacts.

## Requirements for sub-publishing
Each Xbox Sub-Publishing Form contains full guidance on the requirements for sub-publishing. Review this guidance and speak to your Microsoft contacts if you have questions.

> [!NOTE]
> The sub-publisher of your product must have a valid license agreement with Microsoft to publish the product, and the license agreement must be valid for the distribution method being considered. Sub-publishing might require the sub-publisher to submit a [Game Concept](publishing-processes-game-concepts.md), for example if the original Game Concept didn't include any physical publishing or didn't include physical publishing by the primary publisher in a specific market.
