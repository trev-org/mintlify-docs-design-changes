# Product relationship management

The **Product relationship setup** page allows you to specify relationships between your product and other products in Partner Center. You can create a relationship between any products to which you have read or write access in your Partner Center account. 

You can create three types of relationships between products: 
* **can sell**: The related product can be sold from within your game or app. You can set up this type of relationship with other apps or games. 
* **can use and sell**: The related product can function as an add-on to your game or app. You can set up this type of relationship with add-ons of other games or apps.
* **generation of**: The related product functions as a generation of the primary product. You can set up this type of a relationship to specify one product for the Xbox One family of devices and another for the Xbox Series X|S family of devices, such that the correct product is automatically installed by a customer depending on their device.

Relationships between base games and their add-ons are automatically configured when you create an add-on. For this reason, add-ons created under a game are available only on the **Add-ons** page and are unavailable for configuring relationships in that base game. 

## Set up a product relationship

Follow these steps to specify relationships between your products in [Partner Center](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard): 

1. From your **Overview** page, choose the product for which you want to create relationships.   
2. In the navigation menu on the left, select **Product relationship setup**. 
3. On the **Product relationship setup** page, select **Add new relationship**. 
4. In the **Add new relationship** dialog box, select the **Relationship type** dropdown list, and then select the appropriate type according to the product with which you’re creating a relationship.
5. Use the **Filters** section to find the products with which you want to create a relationship. Enter or select your filter criteria, then select **Search**. You can filter available products by name (**Search name**), **Product type**, **Product group**, or **Product ID**. 
6. Select the boxes next to the products you want, and then select **Add**. 
7. After the dialog box closes, select **Save** on the **Product relationship setup** page. If you don’t save your new product relationships, they won't be created. 

After you save your new relationships, a banner will inform you that creating the relationships might take a few minutes. After that time, the relationships will be established and your product will be able to sell (or use and sell) the other products you’ve added. You can view a list of current **Existing relationships** for a product, on its **Product relationship setup** page.

If you want to remove a relationship you’ve previously added, select **Remove** next to its name in the **Existing relationships** section, then select **Save**.
