# Terminology

The Xbox Console Required Terminology List provides developers and publishers with the official terminology required of their games, titles, and applications for Xbox consoles. Branding and marketing guidelines (for example, fonts, packaging templates, standard iconography, and logos) are available on the [Branding and Marketing](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/games/xbox/partner/publishing-xbox-one-brand-assets) site.  


Related terminology documents: 
* [Forbidden Terms List (NDA topic)](/gaming/xbox-nda-docs/_content/gc/policies/console/certification-forbidden-terms)
