---
title: "Multiplayer Activity (MPA)"
description: "Surface activities, invites, and recent players when using a custom matchmaking or lobby system."
---

**Multiplayer Activity (MPA)** lets titles using custom matchmaking, lobbies, or dedicated servers integrate Xbox social experiences — activities (joins), invites, and recent players.

## Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Connection string** | Freeform, title-defined string with whatever's needed to join. Opaque to MPA. |
| **Activity** | Advertisement that a player is in a multiplayer experience. Visible in the shell. |
| **Invite** | A request for a player to join. |
| **Recent player** | Someone who had a meaningful interaction. |

## Client flow

1. Player A starts/joins a multiplayer experience → **set activity**
2. Other players can **retrieve the activity**
3. Player A sends **invites** to other players
4. As players join/leave → **update the activity**
5. Update the **recent players list**
6. When the experience ends → **delete the activity**
