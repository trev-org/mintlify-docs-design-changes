---
title: "Create Xbox test accounts"
description: "Create test accounts in Partner Center to test Xbox services in your development sandbox."
---

Test accounts let you exercise Xbox services in your development sandbox without affecting real player data.

## Steps

1. In [Partner Center](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/), select **Apps and games** → your game
2. From the left nav, select **Xbox services → Xbox test accounts**
3. Select **Create a new account(s)**
4. **Email template** — left side shows the sandbox prefix; right side, enter your game name without spaces
5. **Keywords** — add an identifier
6. **Sandbox access** — select the sandboxes the accounts can use
7. **Adult account batch** — enter a count (e.g., `5`)
8. **Save** — opens the Xbox profile page
9. Enter a **Password**, **Secret question**, **Secret answer**, **Recovery email**
10. **Submit**
11. Wait for the status checkmark, then click the batch name → **View batch accounts** to see emails, gamertags, and XUIDs
