---
title: "Security requirements"
description: "Security obligations all GDK licensees must follow for dev hardware, software, and documentation."
---

The GDK has public and secure portions. The **secure (Partners) GDK** — including dev hardware, software, docs, and related materials — is highly confidential and **must not be shared**.

These terms are part of your **Xbox Development Kit License Agreement** or **Microsoft Game Development Kit License Agreement**.

## Physical access

Unpack, use, and store dev hardware in a **secured, locked location** accessible only to authorized personnel.

## User access

Restrict access (including remote access) to the secure GDK and dev hardware to:

- People with a clear business need
- Employees or approved subcontractors directly involved in the title
- People with confidentiality obligations to your organization

## Asset Custodian

Designate an **Asset Custodian** (a Licensee employee) responsible for:

- Maintaining dev hardware security
- Returning hardware to Microsoft on request
- Scanning **QR code labels** on dev hardware when Microsoft requests verification

QR scans report asset ID, console ID, IP address, and location to a Microsoft-managed cloud service. Used only for security and asset management.

## Confidentiality

- Don't discuss the secure GDK with anyone outside your organization — including family
- Don't post about it on public sites (blogs, newsgroups, forums)
- Forward all media inquiries to Microsoft

## Breach consequences

Microsoft may take any of the following actions on breach:

- Revoke access to the secure GDK
- Terminate the License Agreement and your relationship with Microsoft
- Pursue criminal prosecution

## Audit

Microsoft may audit these security requirements at any time. Violations may constitute a material breach of the Agreement.
