Each GDK release contains features at one of two quality levels.

## Released

Stable, complete, documented, fully supported. Compatible with future recoveries. QFEs do not introduce breaking changes.

## Preview

Close to release; awaiting more testing and developer feedback. Stable and high quality, but:

- Subject to change based on feedback
- Not guaranteed compatible with future recoveries
- Documentation may be minimal
