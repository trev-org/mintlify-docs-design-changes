## Public resources (no authorization)

- [Public gaming documentation](https://learn.microsoft.com/gaming/) — landing page for public gaming docs, including the public subset of GDK documentation
- [Public GDK on GitHub](https://aka.ms/gdk) — common tools, libraries, and docs for Xbox Game Pass for PC, Xbox consoles, and Xbox Cloud Gaming
- [Public GDK samples](https://github.com/microsoft/Xbox-GDK-Samples/) — feature samples

## Secure resources (NDA / Partner only)

Requires onboarding as an Xbox development partner with a valid GDK Agreement:

- [**Secure Xbox downloads**](https://aka.ms/gdkdl) — GDK releases, recoveries, offline docs, Submission Validator, NDA samples (audio, graphics, PlayFab, system, tools, Xbox Live), event materials
- [**Xbox developer forums**](https://forums.xboxlive.com) — direct support from Microsoft
- [**GDK NDA topics**](https://learn.microsoft.com/gaming/xbox-nda-docs/) — Xbox console-specific graphics APIs

## Accessing secure resources

Secure resources require:

1. Membership in an organization with an onboarded Partner Center account
2. A Microsoft Entra tenant associated with that account
3. Your account assigned to the right roles/permissions

<Note>
  Access to Secure Xbox Downloads is **not** managed through Partner Center. Contact your Microsoft representative to enable it.
</Note>

If no Entra tenant is linked yet, see [Set up your organization in Partner Center](/gdk/setup-organization).

<Warning>
  Personal Microsoft Accounts (MSA) are no longer supported. You must use a work account in a Microsoft Entra tenant linked to a Partner Center account.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting access

### Wrong account

Click the account name in the top-right and switch to the work account linked to your organization's Partner Center.

### Wrong Entra tenant

You may belong to multiple tenants:

1. Click your account name (top-right)
2. **Switch directory**
3. Pick the tenant linked to your Partner Center account

If you don't see **Switch directory**, you're signed in with a personal MSA.

### Not added to Partner Center

Being in the Entra tenant doesn't automatically grant Partner Center access. Confirm with your Partner Center account owner that you've been added.
