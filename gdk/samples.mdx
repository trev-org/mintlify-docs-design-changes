The GDK samples library demonstrates how to use the GDK and Xbox extensions across the most common game development scenarios.

## Public samples

[**github.com/microsoft/Xbox-GDK-Samples**](https://github.com/microsoft/Xbox-GDK-Samples) — open-source samples covering:

- D3D12 graphics on PC and Xbox.
- Audio (XAudio2, WASAPI, spatial audio).
- Input (GameInput, XInput).
- Microsoft Store / commerce (`XStore`).
- Connected Storage / Game Saves (`XGameSave`).
- Xbox services (XSAPI): achievements, presence, social, multiplayer.
- Packaging and Intelligent Delivery (`XPackage`).
- PlayFab integration.

## Secure (NDA) samples

Console-specific samples that target Xbox D3D12.x extensions, XMemAlloc, Game Bar / Game DVR, and console-only middleware are available through your **Xbox Game Development Home** portal — sign in with the Microsoft Entra credentials from your Partner Center account.

## Running samples

### PC

1. Open the sample's `.sln` in Visual Studio 2022 / 2026.
2. Pick the **Gaming.Desktop.x64** configuration.
3. Build and **F5** to run on your dev PC.

### Xbox

1. Open the sample's `.sln`.
2. Pick the **Gaming.Xbox.XboxOne.x64** or **Gaming.Xbox.Scarlett.x64** configuration.
3. Connect your dev kit via Xbox Manager.
4. Build and **F5** to deploy and remote-debug.
