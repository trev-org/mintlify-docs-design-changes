This guide walks through every step required to integrate the GDK with your game and publish it to the Microsoft Store on the Xbox PC app. Steps are presented in the order you'd typically perform them.

The guide covers integration with native GDK APIs and includes engine-specific guides for Unity, Unreal, GameMaker, custom C/C\+\+ engines, and Godot.

## Before you start

Complete [**ID@Xbox onboarding**](/onboarding) first. You need an approved game concept, signed agreements, a Partner Center product with Xbox services enabled, and GDK access **before** any of the engine integration steps below will succeed.

## Sections

### 1. GDK & game engines

Describes the GDK and links to engine-specific integration guides for Unity, Unreal, GameMaker, custom C/C\+\+ engines, and Godot.

### 2. Optional services

Xbox services and PlayFab features that aren’t required to publish but are commonly used: chat, in-game purchases, invites, leaderboards, lobbies, matchmaking, multiplayer, networking, player stats, rich presence, service-to-service auth, UGC.

## Which path applies to you

- **C/C\+\+ from scratch** → [Custom engine guide](/pc-e2e/engines/custom-engine) → [Publishing](/publishing/overview) → Optional services
- **Using a game engine** → Engine-specific guide → [Publishing](/publishing/overview) → Optional services
