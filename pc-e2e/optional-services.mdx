description: "Xbox services and PlayFab features that aren't required to publish, but commonly used."

Not every game needs every service. Pick what fits your title.

## Communication

- [**Game chat**](/services/game-chat) — voice \+ text chat across players
- [**Party / PlayFab Party**](/playfab/party-voice) — cross-platform voice \+ data

## Multiplayer

- [**Multiplayer overview**](/services/multiplayer) — sessions, MPSD
- [**Multiplayer Activity (MPA)**](/services/multiplayer-activity) — surface activities in the Xbox shell
- [**PlayFab Multiplayer / Lobby**](/playfab/multiplayer-lobby) — cross-platform lobby \+ matchmaking
- **Invites** — invite friends to your session
- **Matchmaking** — SmartMatch or PlayFab Matchmaking

## Player progression

- [**Leaderboards and stats**](/playfab/leaderboards-stats) — ranked tables, event-based or title-managed stats
- [**Rich Presence**](/services/rich-presence) — show what players are doing

## Commerce

- [**Microsoft Store commerce**](/publishing/commerce) — in-game purchases via `XStore`
- [**PlayFab Economy**](/playfab/economy) — virtual currency, items, catalogs

## Networking and backend

- **Service-to-service authentication** — secure title server auth
- **Networking** — Xbox HTTP, WebSockets
- **User-generated content (UGC)** — hosted player content
