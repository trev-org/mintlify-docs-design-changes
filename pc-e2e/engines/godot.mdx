The [XBOX Godot sample](https://aka.ms/XBOXGodotSample) is a public, MIT-licensed reference for building a Godot extension that integrates the Microsoft GDK, Xbox services, and PlayFab — letting you ship Xbox games on PC from Godot.

## What's in the sample

Working integration for:

- Microsoft GDK platform services
- Xbox services (sign-in, profile, etc.)
- PlayFab Core \+ Services
- PlayFab Multiplayer (Lobby, Matchmaking, Party)
- PlayFab Game Saves
- GameInput (bridged to Godot's Input / InputMap)

Compatible with the **April 2026 GDK** out of the box.

## What it isn't

- **Source-only.** Not a shipped product. GDK and PlayFab still require their own installs and license acceptance.
- **No fixed update cadence.** Microsoft monitors the repo and iterates; not a commercial release. Community PRs welcome.
- **PC only.** Does not include Xbox Series X|S or Xbox One console support.

## Xbox Series X|S and Xbox One support

Not included in this sample. Godot's open-source MIT license is incompatible with the NDA legal requirements of console SDKs — the Godot Foundation doesn't maintain official console export templates.

For console porting paths and middleware providers, see [Godot and console porting](https://godotengine.org/consoles/) on the Godot Engine site.

Already building for Xbox consoles? Contact your Xbox representative. Not registered? See [Onboarding](/onboarding).
