Products must be **certified** before they can be published to the Microsoft Store in the RETAIL sandbox. Use the Partner Center **Certify** workflow to submit individual modules — Packages, Store listings, Pricing and availability — for review.

## Which modules need certification

| Module | Reviewer | Typical SLA |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Packages** | Xbox Certification team (XR testing) | Varies by platform and submission type — see the [certification guide](#cert-guide) |
| **Store listings** | Store editorial / art review | ~1 business day |
| **Pricing and availability** | Pricing review | ~5 business days |

Each module can be certified independently, in any branch. Approved content must be imported to the **Main** branch before publishing to RETAIL. Only one branch per module can be in cert at a time.

## Prepare for certification

Every module must be **complete** before submission. If you publish via [Review and Publish](#) before a required module is certified, cert reviews are generated automatically and the publish blocks until they're approved.

### Packages

Packages are tested for compliance with the [Xbox Requirements (XRs)](#).

Before submitting packages, you must complete:

1. **Certification questionnaire** — from your product's page, select **Certification** in the navigation.
2. **Supplemental files** (optional) — upload walkthroughs, known-issues docs, or achievements guides via **Files** in the product navigation.

### Store listings

Store listing cert covers:

- Art assets (logos, screenshots, trailers, hero art)
- Text elements (title, descriptions, feature bullets)

See [Store listings](/publishing/store-listing) for asset requirements.

### Pricing and availability

Certifying pricing generates a review of base price, market currencies, sale schedules, and release dates.

## Add-ons

Durable, Durable-with-packages, and Consumable add-ons must also be certified, but they don't support module-by-module submission. Submit the whole add-on for cert from its **Overview** page.

<Tip>
  If you're submitting an add-on to support a base-game cert and not all info is finalized, fill the minimum required sections to mark each page **Complete**, then explain in **Notes to Certification** which sections are placeholders. Update and resubmit once finalized.
</Tip>

## Notes to Certification

When submitting via **Certify**, you can add notes. Use this field for context the test team needs — known issues, test account credentials, workflow guidance for hidden content.

## After certification

Results appear in Partner Center. If your submission **passes**, the module is ready to publish to RETAIL. If it **fails**, a certification report describes the issues. Resolve them and resubmit via **Certify**.
