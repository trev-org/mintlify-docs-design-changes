---
title: "Xbox Requirements for Console games"
description: "The policies, technical, and product-component requirements every Xbox console title must meet."
---

**Xbox Requirements (XRs)** are the policies all Xbox console games must meet. XRs tested in Certification are marked with `*`.

## Base requirements

| XR | Title | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **XR-001** \* | Title Stability | Start promptly, remain responsive, shut down gracefully |
| **XR-003** \* | Title Quality for Submission | Functionally complete \+ testable. Clean Submission Validator |
| **XR-130** \* | Console Families and Generations | Support entire family per generation |
| **XR-022** \* | Official Naming Standards | No references to competing platforms |
| **XR-074** \* | Loss of Connectivity | Gracefully handle service errors |
| **XR-132** \* | Service Access Limitations | Stay within rate limits |
| **XR-133** \* | Local Storage Write Limitations | ≤ 1 GiB per 5 minutes |
