Cert Bypass is an opt-in program that lets approved titles ship **minor updates** to a retail product without going through the full certification process. For live-service teams running weekly hotfixes, it can collapse the patch-to-retail timeline from days to hours.

<Note>
  Cert Bypass is a privilege for titles in good standing, not a default. Eligibility is reviewed per title, and abuse (shipping a cert-failing build through bypass) can result in being removed from the program.
</Note>

## Eligibility

To qualify, your title typically needs to:

- Have shipped to retail and been live in the store for a minimum probationary period.
- Have a low certification-fail rate on recent submissions.
- Be operated by a team with a Microsoft point of contact in the Managed Partner program.
- Demonstrate a robust internal QA process.

Apply through Partner Center under **Publishing processes** → **Cert Bypass**. Microsoft reviews the application and approves the title (not the studio) for bypass on a per-product basis.

## What you can ship under bypass

Allowed:

- Bug fixes that do not change ratings-impacting content.
- Server-side balance tweaks delivered through your back end (Title Storage, PlayFab, CloudScript).
- Small content drops within the existing rated scope.
- Critical security patches.

**Not** allowed:

- New age-ratings-impacting content (added violence, new in-game purchases, new chat surfaces).
- New market launches.
- Major engine or platform upgrades.
- DRM or anti-cheat changes that affect retail experience.

When in doubt: submit normally.

## Workflow

1. Build, sign, and validate the package locally with [SubmissionValidator](/tools/xbox-service-tools).
2. Upload to Partner Center and mark the submission as a **Cert Bypass** update.
3. Microsoft performs an automated check (scan for obvious cert violations).
4. If the automated check passes, the build flows directly to retail — typically within hours.
5. If anything trips the automated check, the build falls back to full certification.

## Maintaining good standing

Keep your bypass privilege by:

- Running every bypass candidate through your own internal cert suite first.
- Treating bypass like production. The same release-engineering rigor applies.
- Reverting fast. If a bypass build causes player-impacting issues, ship a follow-up bypass within 24 hours rather than letting the issue persist.
