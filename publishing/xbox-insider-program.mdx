The **Xbox Insider Program** is the audience-targeted public preview channel for Xbox titles. You publish a flight build to an opt-in audience of Insider players, collect feedback and crash data, then promote the same packages to retail once they're stable.

## When to use Insider flighting

- **Open betas** — the gold standard public-beta channel for Xbox titles.
- **Multiplayer load tests** — invite a known cohort of Insiders to validate matchmaking and dedicated server capacity at scale before launch.
- **Major update validation** — ship a release candidate to Insiders for a week before promoting to retail.
- **Regional soft launch** — target Insiders in a specific country to validate localization and payment flows.

## How flighting works

1. Build your package as you normally would for cert.
2. In Partner Center, create a **flight** under the Packages module and attach the package.
3. Choose an **audience** — either an Insider Program audience (public opt-in) or an internal email-list audience for closed tests.
4. Submit the flight for review. Cert is lighter-weight than a retail submission but is still required.
5. Once approved, the flight is live for the selected audience. Promote it to retail when ready, or roll it back if you find issues.

## Insider Program audiences

The Insider Program organizes players into rings (Alpha, Beta, Delta, etc.) by signup tier and platform. You target one or more rings when creating a flight. Players in the matching ring see a join offer in the Xbox Insider Hub app.

Don't ship a build to a public Insider audience until you would be willing to publish it to retail. Insider players talk publicly and stream gameplay; treat the build as effectively launched.
