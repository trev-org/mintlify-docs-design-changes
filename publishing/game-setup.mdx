Use the **Game setup** page (or **Bundle setup** for bundles) to configure top-level product options that drive every other Partner Center module.

## Product group

Applies to: **Game, Game Demo, Game Bundle**.

Product groups organize related products in the **Apps and Games** dashboard.

Create a product group:

1. **Apps and Games** dashboard → **New Product** → **Product group**.
2. Set **Product group name**, **Dev studio** (create new if needed), and the products to include.
3. **Save**.

Assign a product to a group from its Game setup page → select group → **Save draft**.

## Content type

Applies to: **Durable, Durable with Packages, Consumable**.

Usually **Electronic software download**.

## Microsoft Store listing

Applies to: **Durable, Durable with Packages, Consumable, Season Pass, Add-on Bundle**.

Indicate whether the product has its own Store listing. In-game-only purchases may not need one.

<Note>
  Game, Game Bundle, and Game Demo products **must** have a Store listing.
</Note>

## Device family availability

Applies to: **All product types**.

For each device family (Xbox, Desktop), select:

- **Retail** — required to publish to RETAIL. Automatically also selects Sandboxes.
- **Sandboxes** — required to publish to dev sandboxes or submit for cert.

If neither is checked for a device family, you can't upload packages for it.

## Xbox services

Applies to: **Game, Game Demo**.

- **Limited Xbox services** are enabled the first time you save Game setup. Limited means **no achievements**.
- **Full Xbox services** (including achievements) are enabled for Managed Creators with an approved game concept after Microsoft onboards the product.

If you use **game binding** to link two products' Xbox services, the secondary product's Xbox services stay disabled.

## Sandboxes

The sandboxes selection (set when creating the product) determines whether the product uses content isolation. **You can't change this later.** See [Sandboxes](/publishing/sandboxes).

### Primary Xbox services development sandbox

Set the primary dev sandbox — this adds a branch to your Xbox services Gameplay settings. To use the product in other sandboxes, select **Manage sandboxes**.

<Note>
  You must publish the product to a dev sandbox before any Partner Center features for that sandbox become testable.
</Note>

## Included products

Applies to: **Game Bundle, Add-on Bundle, Season Pass**.

Products must be **published to the Store** before they can be added to a bundle submitted for cert.

Add products:

- **Manage included products** — search and pick multiple.
- **Add products by Store ID** — enter the Store ID (found on each product's setup page → Identity Details).

For each included product, set **Visible in bundle**:

| Setting | When the product is visible in the bundle |
| --- | --- |
| at bundle release | When the bundle releases (per its Pricing and availability Schedule) |
| at custom date / time | A custom date and time |
| never | Never visible in the bundle |

<Warning>
  **Visible in bundle** only controls visibility inside the bundle. Customers can still find the product via its own Pricing and availability Schedule.
</Warning>

## Identity details

Shows the identifiers needed in your MicrosoftGame.config and elsewhere:

- **Store ID**
- **Legacy product ID**
- **Xbox Title ID**
- **Xbox Service Configuration ID (SCID)**
- **MSAAppID**
- **Package Identity Name** and **Publisher**

Also includes:

- **Store protocol link** — works in RETAIL or dev sandboxes; opens the Store app directly to your PDP.
- **Store URL** — RETAIL only, after the product is published.
