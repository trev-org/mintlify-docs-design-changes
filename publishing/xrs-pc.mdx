---
title: "Xbox Requirements for PC, Mobile, and Creators Program"
description: "XRs for adding Xbox network features to PC, mobile, or another console."
---

These requirements apply when Xbox network services are integrated into a **PC, mobile, or non-Xbox console** game.

XRs marked `*` are tested in Certification (Microsoft Store PC submissions). PC games on other stores are monitored — failure may result in **revocation of Xbox network access**.

| XR | Title |
| --- | --- |
| **XR-001** \* | Title Stability |
| **XR-003** \* | Title Quality for Submission |

| **XR-022** \* | Official Naming Standards |
| **XR-074** \* | Loss of Connectivity |
| **XR-132** \* | Service Access Limitations |

## Player data and privacy

Use data only to deliver your game. Don’t sell / license / share with third parties. Don’t share with ad networks. Check the [Deleted Account List](https://forums.xboxlive.com/articles/78346/deleted-account-list-tools-for-xbox-live.html) at least every **30 days**.
