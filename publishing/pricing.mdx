Pricing and availability is where you decide **what your game costs, where it's sold, and when it shows up**. Partner Center exposes a single Pricing and availability page that controls all of it for the base game and every add-on.

## Base price and market currencies

Set the base price in USD; the store automatically generates equivalent local-currency prices for every enabled market using current Microsoft Store currency tiers. You can override individual markets if you need a region-specific price (common for Brazil, Turkey, Russia, and India).

```text
Base price (USD):        $59.99
Germany override (EUR):  €59.99
UK override (GBP):       £49.99
Brazil override (BRL):   R$249.99
```

## Markets

By default a new product is enabled in every supported market. Exclude markets where you don't have rights, where your content can't be rated, or where regulatory considerations apply. Common exclusions:

- Mainland China (separate licensing path)
- Markets where your age rating couldn't be issued
- Markets where music or font licensing is region-restricted

Market changes after launch are allowed but slow — plan the initial list carefully.

## Release date and discoverability

Three controls work together:

| Setting | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Release date** | The earliest the product becomes purchasable |
| **Visibility** | Public, hidden, or available only via direct link |
| **Discoverability** | Show in store browse and search, or hide from discovery while remaining purchasable via link |

Use hidden \+ direct-link for private beta builds, then flip to public on launch day.

## Wish List pages

Before release, configure a Wish List page so players can mark interest. Wish-listers receive a launch-day notification and — if you enable it — a discount on the day the game becomes available. Wish List counts are a strong signal of pre-launch demand.

## Pre-orders

If you want to take real-money pre-orders rather than just Wish List signups, configure a pre-order release date and price. Pre-orders unlock pre-loading of the game package on the player's console so the title is playable the moment release-time hits.

## Sales and discounts

Schedule a sale by adding a discount with a start and end date. You can layer multiple sales (storewide promotion \+ your own seasonal sale) and target specific markets.

## Xbox Game Pass

If Microsoft has agreed to bring your title to Game Pass, the catalog and availability controls in Partner Center include a separate flow for Game Pass entitlements. Talk to your representative — you can't self-add a title to Game Pass through the UI.
