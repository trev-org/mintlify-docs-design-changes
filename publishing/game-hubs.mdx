A **Game Hub** is the per-title page that lives at the top of the Xbox dashboard, in the guide, and on Xbox.com. It is the canonical place players go to see their friends' activity in your title, browse the achievement list, watch clips, read your latest news, and launch the game.

A **Franchise Game Hub** ties multiple titles together — useful for sequels, spinoffs, and content updates that ship as separate SKUs.

## What you can author

From Partner Center, populate the Game Hub with:

- **News posts.** First-party blog posts shown in the hub timeline and pushed as notifications to followers.
- **Title art and branding.** Background image, logo, color theme.
- **Pinned activity.** Highlight a specific achievement, clip, or event.
- **Showcase clips.** Curate community clips that represent the title well.

Players see this content as soon as they navigate to your title in the dashboard, even before launching the game.

## Franchise hubs

For a multi-title franchise (a trilogy, an annual sports series, a live game with multiple expansions), create a Franchise Game Hub that:

- Aggregates news across every title in the franchise.
- Lets players follow the franchise as a whole, not just individual titles.
- Surfaces all of the franchise's titles together in one navigation surface.

Request a Franchise Hub through your Microsoft contact in Managed Partners. You provide the franchise branding and the list of titles to include.

## News post best practices

- **Headline first.** The card in the dashboard shows only the first ~60 characters.
- **One topic per post.** Don't bundle a patch announcement with a community spotlight; ship them separately.
- **Cadence matters.** Follower notifications fire on every post. Weekly is healthy; daily can feel like spam.
- **Localize.** Like store listings, news posts need per-language translations for every market that gets the notification.

## Linking activity

Game Hubs surface friend activity automatically when your title properly publishes:

- [Achievements](/services/achievements)
- [Rich Presence](/services/rich-presence)
- [Multiplayer Activity](/services/multiplayer-activity) joinable sessions
- Game DVR clips and screenshots

The more of these signals your title emits, the richer the Game Hub feels.
