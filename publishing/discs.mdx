If you ship your title on **physical disc** (Xbox Series X and Xbox One disc-equipped consoles), you manage the disc relationship through Partner Center alongside the digital release.

## Disc request management

Disc orders flow through the **Discs** section of your title in Partner Center:

- Configure the **disc image** by uploading the master package the disc factory will replicate.
- Submit a **disc layout file** (`.xml`) that tells the replicator how to lay out the data, multi-disc spans, and stamping metadata.
- Track the order status from approval through replication to fulfillment.

Disc submissions go through certification just like digital packages. Plan for an extra month of lead time over the digital lane.

## Multi-disc and cross-gen discs

Large titles that span multiple discs, or that ship a cross-gen disc with both Xbox One and Xbox Series X|S binaries, declare the layout in the disc layout file. Keep the digital and disc package versions in lock-step so day-one updates are minimal.

## Royalty configuration

Disc royalties are configured separately from digital pricing because they include physical-media costs (replication, packaging, distribution). Set the royalty configuration before submitting the first disc order; you can revise it for future re-pressings.

## Day-one updates

Most disc titles ship a day-one update over the network because cert \+ replication takes weeks. Plan the on-disc build to be the **safe minimum** (boots, navigates menus, displays the update prompt) and rely on the update for shipping features that landed late.
