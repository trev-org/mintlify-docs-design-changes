The **Properties** module configures basic info, technical capabilities, and the Xbox services your game supports. These values appear on the Microsoft Store Product Details Page (PDP).

## Basic info

Required for every game:

- **Genre**
- **Support contacts** (email, URL)
- **Privacy information**
- **Gameplay modes** (single-player, online co-op, etc.)
- **Accessibility features**

## Technical capabilities

Required for every game. Drives the Store's PDP highlights and discovery filters:

### Xbox product-page highlights

- **Optimized for Xbox Series X|S**
- **Available in 4K**
- **Smart Delivery**
- **Xbox Play Anywhere**

### Audio/visual

- **HDR**
- **Ray Tracing**
- **Dolby Atmos**

### Input devices

Supported controllers, keyboard/mouse, peripherals.

### PC requirements

- Minimum specifications
- Recommended specifications

## Xbox services

Declare your support for:

- **Achievements**
- **Cloud saves**
- **Cross-gen multiplayer**
- **Rich Presence**
- **Clubs**

What you mark here must match your actual implementation — Microsoft validates these claims during certification.
