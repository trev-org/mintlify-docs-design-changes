Microsoft PlayFab is a complete backend-as-a-service (BaaS) platform designed to help you build, launch, and grow live games. Whether you need robust player identity management, a flexible in-game economy, real-time multiplayer infrastructure, or deep analytics, PlayFab provides the managed services that let your team focus on gameplay rather than server operations. PlayFab integrates directly with Xbox services and the Microsoft GDK, making it the natural choice for titles shipping on Xbox and Windows.

## Supported SDKs and platforms

PlayFab meets you where your game is built. The following languages, platforms, and game engines all have first-class SDK support.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Languages">
    - **C/C\+\+** — native SDK for GDK, console, and high-performance titles
    - **C#** — ideal for Unity and .NET-based game servers
    - **Java** — Android and server-side integrations
    - **JavaScript** — browser and Node.js environments
    - **Python** — tooling, automation, and back-end services
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Platforms">
    - **Android**
    - **iOS**
    - **macOS**
    - **Linux**
    - **Microsoft GDK** (Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, Windows PC)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Game Engines">
    - **Unity 3D** — plugin available from the GDK download site
    - **Unreal Engine** — Online Subsystem plugin included with the GDK
    - **Cocos2D-x** — supported via the JavaScript or C\+\+ SDK
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Pricing

Creating a PlayFab title is free for all developers. Costs may apply when you launch a live title based on monthly active users (MAUs) and feature usage. Review the full pricing breakdown before you ship.
