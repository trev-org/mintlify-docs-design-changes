---
title: "Player sign-in concepts (PlayFab + GDK)"
description: "How Xbox and PlayFab accounts link together when a player signs in to your GDK title."
---

GDK titles that use PlayFab must authenticate players against **both** Xbox services and PlayFab.

## PlayFab titles

Before your game can call PlayFab services, create a title in the [PlayFab Game Manager](https://developer.playfab.com). You'll get a **PlayFab TitleId** (4–6 hex digits).

<Warning>
  The **PlayFab TitleId is not the same as your Xbox Title ID**.
</Warning>

## Xbox vs PlayFab accounts

Two separate accounts:

- **Xbox services account** — player-facing identity
- **PlayFab account** — behind-the-scenes identity for PlayFab services, linked to the Xbox account in the context of your title

By the end of sign-in:

1. Each player has a PlayFab account provisioned in your title
2. The Xbox and PlayFab accounts are linked to each other
3. The player is authenticated with both services

## Two ways to sign in

| Option | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| **PlayFab Services SDK** | Recommended for any title using PlayFab services |
| **PlayFab Party Xbox Live Helper Library** | Use only if PlayFab Party is your **only** PlayFab service AND Xbox is your only auth provider |
